Preemptive warning to prevent food allergy

Problem Statement

As a user when I order food from apps like Doordash or Ubereats, I am not aware if I accidentally ordered food which I might be allergic to.

Goal

As a user I should get an alert beforehand if I'm allergic to the food that I'm ordering.

Actions

  • We will take user's allergy details at the time of creation of their profile. We will save the allergy data and associate it with the user.
  • When restaurant is uploading their menu, we need to know food content so that we can show user correct ingredients.
  • If the user is ordering the food that they are allergic to then they will get a warning. The warning will let user know that they are allergic to the food they are about to order. User would have a choice whether to change order or ignore the warning.

Potential obstacles

  • When user is providing allergy data we need to make sure that their personal health data is saved in highly encrypted database and product is GDPR compliant.
  • Restaurant may not provide enough information and therefore we may need to hire a nutritionist who curates the food and its contents. This process can be tedious for a region with large number of restaurants.

Impact

  • Reduce the potential risk of food poisoning among users.
  • Reduction of food wastage.
